After manually matching entries, sometimes the reason why bank statement entries are unmatched is because the corresponding entries have not been entered into G-Fast yet.

Follow the steps below to manually create an entry in G-Fast for each outstanding bank statement transaction :

STEP 1
Click on 'Create a new entry' button. Enter the payment or receipt details on the pop up.
STEP 2
The new entry entered should be the same amount as the bank statement transaction on the left.
STEP 3
Continue until all bank transactions on the left are accounted for.
