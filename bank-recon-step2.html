In this step, we will manually match entries in your bank statement to entries in G-FAST. Sometimes, bank statement transactions might comprise of multiple entries in G-Fast. Follow the steps below to manually match these entries :

STEP 1
In the Bank Statement section on the left, select a transaction.
STEP 2
In the G-Fast Entries section on the right, check off one or more entries that match.
STEP 3
Click on the Match Button. Do it for as many bank statement transactions as you can.
STEP 4
Click on next to proceed.
